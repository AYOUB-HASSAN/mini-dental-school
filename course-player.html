Maxillary Central Incisor Anatomy

About this lesson

In this lesson, we cover the essential anatomy of the Maxillary Central Incisor. We will look at its labial, lingual, mesial, distal, and incisal aspects, identifying key landmarks such as the cingulum, marginal ridges, and mamelons.
